About Me

My Story

Backend developer with over two years of Python and Django experience, skilled in building scalable web applications, designing RESTful APIs, optimizing database-driven applications (PostgreSQL, MySQL, SQLite), creating interactive dashboards, and automating data-processing and reporting workflows. Comfortable leading Agile sprints in Jira and collaborating with cross-functional teams. An engineering background provides a structured, analytical approach to delivering maintainable, high-performance systems.

I enjoy the problem-solving side of backend development especially turning complex ideas into clean, efficient systems. I'm always exploring new tools, refining my workflow, and challenging myself to write better, more maintainable code.

I hold a Master's degree in Industrial Engineering with a focus on Project Management from University of Tehran. I speak English, Turkish, and Farsi, which helps me connect and collaborate with teams from diverse backgrounds and perspectives.
